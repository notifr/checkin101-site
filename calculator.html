Pricing

Notifr has a simple pricing structure

We don't limit you by the number of people in your organisation, classes or communities. Instead we charge on a per-check-in basis (each time you check-in with one of your segments). Since we build discounts into our payment structure, your per-check-in pricing automatically decreases the more you check-in. Run a check-in for as many people as you want through segments.

Example volumes:

  • 60 check-ins/year Allow 5 of your staff to check-in monthly with their teams or programmes.
  • 500 check-ins/year Your organisation can run weekly check-ins for 5 different segments.
  • 1,200 check-ins/year Gives 20 of your staff
  • 3,800 check-ins/year Run bi-weekly check-ins with 200 segments or classes!
