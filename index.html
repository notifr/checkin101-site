How It Works


Team leaders schedule check-ins at regular intervals, or when they're needed most.


Teams are reminded to answer a few relevant questions, without feeling like they're being watched over.

Ask the questions that matter to you

Check-in are flexible so you can ask your teams about what you want. Unlike 360 feedback, check-ins are designed for targeted audiences rather than a one-size-fits-all survey.

Questions that help you make a difference

Check-in questions are automatically suggested based on what you want to check-in about. Get started quickly and spend your time understanding what's working, not thinking of the perfect question.

Take action at the first sign of trouble

Quickly identify your team champions, as well as the people at risk in your teams. Check-in throughout the month and get a better gauge on how you can create a high performing, happy team.

Easily check-in, whenever you need to

Get started by scheduling check-ins through your own calendar. Get them to coincide with meetings, create recurring events or send a check-in whenever you want to know how your team is going.
